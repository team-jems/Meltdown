Welcome to the Meltdown!

















Fix the reactor - cool down the core - or face total meltdown!

Hack your way through the levels with a group of engineers (or go solo)! Fix the broken parts of the nuclear reactor by solving JavaScript puzzles. When on team, you can't do it alone, so depend on your team and remember - don't melt down!


GAME PLAY

Solo (or) Team of 2 - 4

Click play to head into the reactor. When you're ready for the madness, join the game. Hold down the go button at the same time as your friends to be sent into level-1 where you have the reactor manual on the right and game room on the left. The manual is the only way to fix the room's broken part. It consists of names for broken module's you and your team of engineers are working on as well as JavaScript code to be interpreted to find a fix. Your manual is only partially legible though, so you must work together to fill in the missing pieces.

In this game, nobody gets left behind - only when everyone is done can you all move together to next level to tackle a new batch of increasingly difficult challenges. The reactor only has a limited tolerance for bugs, so watch your strikeout count and don't mess up - or melt down - under the pressure! Oh yeah, you have a time limit. When the time runs out - the reactor meltsdown, BOOM! Game Over.

This is a game of communication so you need to be in the same place as your friends or hop on a hangout or another form of voice conference to successfully beat the heat and win the game. Enjoy!
