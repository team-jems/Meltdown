Welcome to the Meltdown!











Fix the reactor to cool down the core before total meltdown!

Hack your way through the levels with a group of engineers (your friends)! Fix the broken parts of the nuclear reactor by solving javascript puzzles. You can't do it alone though, so depend on your team and remember - don't melt down!


GAME PLAY

2 - 4 players

Click play to head into the reactor. When you're ready for the madness, join the game. Hold down the go button at the same time as your friends to be sent into level 1 where you have the reactor manual on the right and your game room on the left. This manual is the only way to fix your room's broken part. It consists of the names for the puzzle you and your team of engineers are working on as well as the javascript code you must solve in order to get through the level. Your manual is only partially legible though, so you must work together to fill in the missing pieces. Explore the room (using arrow keys) to find the broken part.

In this game, nobody gets left behind - only when everyone is done can you all move to the next level together to solve a new batch of increasingly difficult puzzles. You have a limited amount of mistakes so watch your strikeout count and don't mess up - or melt down - under the pressure! Oh yeah, and you have a time limit. When the time runs out - the reactor overheats and BOOM! . Game Over .

This is a game of communication so you need to be in the same place as your friends or hop on a hangout or another form of voice conference to successfully beat the heat and win the game!
